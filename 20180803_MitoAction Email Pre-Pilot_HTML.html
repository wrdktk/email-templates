MitoAction Mobile Piloteers!

 
It’s almost time. Next week you will embark on a journey that will help revolutionize the care and understanding of mitochondrial disease. Before we go any further, we want to THANK YOU for all you do and for participating in this groundbreaking project.
 
You will receive a Webex email inviting you to your training session. Please add this to your calendar and follow the instructions to join during your scheduled time. 

What To Do Before Your Training

  • Install the Care3 mobile app from the App Store or Google Play

  • Sign up by using the same email address where you received this message. Once you complete the signup process, you will automatically enter the MitoAction Mobile version of Care3.
  • Check out our Glossary so you can learn key terms, e.g., Care Team, Action Plan, Action Messages, Symptoms, Vitals, and more.
  • Review the App and Action Planning training decks to become familiar. We will go over these during your training session.
That’s it! We’re very excited to take this huge step in helping you and changing the course of mitochondrial disease care.
