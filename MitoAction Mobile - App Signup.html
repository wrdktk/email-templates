Welcome to MitoAction Mobile
Welcome to MitoAction Mobile powered by Care3! The one truth we know at MitoAction is that everyone is better when we care together. Here are three things to get started.

Step 1: Start Texting Right Away

On the home Conversations screen you will see your Care Team Conversation. Text everyone “hello” to announce that you’re in and ready to go.

Step 2: Accept Actions  

When you enter the Care Team Conversation, you will see care tasks (Actions) that you accept as a promise to complete. Simply tap the red 'accept' button for all of the Actions you're supposed to do.
 
Preparation complete. You're ready!

Step 3: View and Complete Actions in My Actions Calendar

All of your accepted Actions are added to My Actions Calendar. Tap the calendar icon on the home 'Conversations' screen to view your Actions to-do list by date.
 
When you complete an Action, let everyone know! Tap on the Action from the list and tap the ‘done’ button at the bottom of the screen. This notifies the Care Team that you completed the task. Well done!
