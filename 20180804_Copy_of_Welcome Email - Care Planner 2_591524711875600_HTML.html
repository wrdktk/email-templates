Welcome to Care3!

Care3 will help you quickly and easily organize care tasks (Actions) and organize your Care Team. Each Action is sent to your Care Team members as Action Messages via the Care3 mobile app.

 

Step 1: Create Your Care Plan

Your Care Plan sets the stage for success. Tap 'Create a New Care Plan'. Simply follow the 8 Steps to Success one at a time. It's that easy.

You will add all Care Team members including family caregivers and all care tasks to be completed. Care3 automatically invites everyone to download the Care3 mobile app when you complete the Care Plan. The Care Team members will accept the Actions they will complete. We said it was easy!

 

Step 2: Join The Conversation

Once you send your care plan, make sure to download the mobile app to your iOS or Android device, or login to Care3 Web Messaging (beta) to join the care conversation (see App Store and Google Play links below). Simply login using your email and password.

 

Step 3: Manage Notifications

Your Care Team gets things done. But sometimes Actions may fall through the cracks and not be complete. Care3 Notifications alert you when an Action isn't complete on time. Simply tap the 'bell' icon on the left navigation to view any Actions that were missed and their status. You can send a message to the Care Team member responsible to follow-up. 

Care3 lets you know when care isn't being delivered to intervene BEFORE an emergency visit or hospitalization occurs.
